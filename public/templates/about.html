Welcome to Reagan Match

ReaganMatch is a website where students register and answer a questionnaire, which divides into simple questions and special questions. As you answer the special questions, you assign a level of importance. Then, we match up your answers with other students through a special algorithm. The Reagan Match application returns the results based on your interests and we show in your account the best and worst ten matches of students from your grade and the whole school.

In order to see your profile with your matches, you will have to pay to the Calculus Club during lunch.
